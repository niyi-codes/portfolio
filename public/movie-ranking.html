The Best Movies According to Victor

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jumong

I can say this is the best Korean adventure movie i've seen so far,
The story line teaches one never to give up on himself and keep fighting
for what is rightfully yours.

Merlin

Merlin is an amazing movie that i can tag whitehat & blackhat magical movie.
The storyline is about a young boy that shoulders the destiny of a great
kingdom, and the safety of it's people against ever forces that tends to use their
magic powers in the wrong direction.

The Bee Keeper

The movie is by far the best action movie i've seen so far. It is about a man
that is fighting to avenge the death of a woman that commited suicide after
was scammed by an agency that belongs to the son of a corrupt US president.
This is a movie to watch anytime anyday.
